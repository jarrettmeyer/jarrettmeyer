What does it mean to "repair the world"?

If you see what needs to be repaired and you know how to repair it, then you have found a piece of the world that God has left for you to complete. But if you only see what it wrong and what is ugly in the world, then it is you yourself that needs repair.

— Menachem Mendel Schneerson

Why Don't Jews Proselytize?

(That means seek converts)

  1. There is no requirement that someone be Jewish to be a good person
  2. Jews do not share the same ideas of heaven and salvation of other religions
  3. Jews have been banned from seeking converts in most countries for most of history

Project #1

In groups of 2 or 3...

  • Come up with a list of 10 things that you believe need to be repaired
  • They can be physical, spiritual, societal, political, emotional, educational, local, global, ...
  • Your list should be a good mix of big things and little things
  • We may know how to solve them or we may not
  • Everyone in your group needs a copy of your list
  • Do not work ahead

Project #2

In different groups of 2 or 3...

  • Pick 10 items from your combined list...
  • Who needs to do the repairing?
  • Answers might be: everyone, you, me, your family, clergy (rabbis & cantors), politicians, people of different faiths...
  • Your answers will depend on the items you pick

Project #3

In different groups of 2 or 3...

Pick 5 items from your combined list...

List a few of key steps necessary for repair

If your repair steps are simple (3 or fewer steps), what are some steps to keep your item in consistent good repair?

How wonderful it is that no one need wait a single moment to start to improve the world.

— Anne Frank, 26 March 1944

Project #4

By yourself...

  • What can you do?
  • Pick 1 item from all the lists you've been a part of
  • Something you can have an impact on quickly (low hanging fruit)
  • How can you do it?
